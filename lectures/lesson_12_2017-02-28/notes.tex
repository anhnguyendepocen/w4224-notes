A handy piece of advice.

\begin{equation}
	\left( \giventhat*{y}{x} \right) \sim N\left( x, \sigma ^2 \right), x \sim N\left( \mu, \tau ^2 \right) \Longrightarrow y \sim N\left( \mu, \sigma ^2 + \tau ^2 \right)
\end{equation}

The alternative to cross-validation is the so-called information criteria
